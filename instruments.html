The Collection

An archive of endangered sound.

Four instruments so far — each one recorded on location, voice by voice, and rebuilt as a playable instrument — for KONTAKT and as a standalone app. Every entry leads to the full story.

No. 01 · Pipe Organ · 1900s

Historic Organ

A late-romantic German pipe organ, sampled register by register in the church it has inhabited for over a century — with the room’s own acoustics built in. Play all 17 stops live in the browser, then take the full instrument home.

17 stops · Multi-mic · 24-bit / 96 kHz · KONTAKT 8+

Transistor Organ

A 1960s home organ on slender steel legs: rocker tabs, built-in vibrato, and a tone somewhere between reed organ and space age. Designed as living-room furniture, remembered on garage and lounge records.

Rocker tabs · Vibrato on + off · 24-bit / 96 kHz · KONTAKT 8+

Reed Piano
